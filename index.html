About Me

Zach White is an upcoming Computer Science graduate from the University of Pittsburgh. He is most experienced working with web applications and database management, including both front-end web design and database-integrated back-end design. He is also interested in media composition, working with both audio and visual editing programs such as Ableton, Audacity, and Adobe Premiere. Zach enjoys analyzing artistic media, especially video games, and prides himself on being somewhat of a walking video game encyclopedia.
