Skills Update 2: 3/2/18

Last week, I had said that my focus on Audition and voice editing was perhaps a little limiting in terms of what I can do in 20 hours. Having felt satisfied with my work with Audition, I used this week to learn about synthesizers.

I have gathered more materials, this time for the combination of Ableton Live’s “operator” synthesizer to begin with, and then the Massive synthesizer plugin for Ableton. I was very pleased with what I was able to accomplish this week. At the end of this week, I was able to incorporate the operator into a beat that I had previously made, and I understand the basics of creating a custom synthesizer sound, even if I am bad at it. At the very least, I now can understand how the presets were created.
